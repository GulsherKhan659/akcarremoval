Common Questions

  • How does your car removal process work?

    Our 3-step process is simple: 1) Get an instant quote via phone or online form. 2) Schedule free pickup at your convenience. 3) Receive cash payment when we collect your vehicle. No paperwork hassles - we handle all ownership transfers.

  • When will I receive payment for my vehicle?

    You'll receive cash on the spot when we collect your car. For bank transfers, payments clear within 2 business hours. We never delay payment - over 98% of customers receive immediate cash at pickup.

  • What documents do I need to provide?

    Just proof of ownership (registration or WoF documents). No need for current registration - we accept expired documents. If you've lost paperwork, we can help verify ownership through NZTA records.

  • Is towing really free?

    Absolutely! Our free towing service covers all Auckland suburbs. Whether your vehicle is in your driveway, parking building, or roadside, we'll remove it at no cost to you - we even cover parking fees if required.

  • How do you handle environmental regulations?

    We're NZTA-certified recyclers. All vehicles are processed at our approved facility where 95%+ materials are recycled. Hazardous fluids are disposed following EPA guidelines, and we provide recycling certificates on request.

Our Service

  • Do you offer same-day service?

    Yes! We operate 24/7 including public holidays. Most urban Auckland areas get same-day pickup when booked before 3pm. Rural areas typically within 24 hours - we service all North Island locations.

  • How do your prices compare?

    We guarantee to beat any written quote by 10%. Our pricing considers current scrap metal rates, vehicle condition, and market demand. On average, customers receive 20-30% more than other Auckland buyers.

  • What vehicles do you accept?

    All makes, models and conditions: cars, trucks, vans, 4WDs, motorbikes. Running or not - we buy flood-damaged, accident vehicles, and even cars without engines. No vehicle is too old or damaged!

  • Is there any obligation after getting a quote?

    No obligation whatsoever. Our quotes are valid for 7 days. You pay nothing - we pay you. Over 85% of quotes result in completed pickups thanks to our transparent pricing.

  • Can you remove insurance written-off vehicles?

    Yes, we specialize in insurance total loss cases. We coordinate directly with your insurer, handle all paperwork, and ensure quick settlement. Many insurance companies recommend our service.

Vehicle Requirements

  • Do you buy cars with mechanical issues?

    Absolutely! We purchase vehicles in any condition - blown engines, transmission failures, electrical problems. In fact, non-running cars often get top value due to salvageable parts.

  • What if my car doesn't start?

    No problem! Our tow trucks come equipped with winches and dollies. We can remove vehicles stuck in garages, on lawns, or without wheels. Just inform us about the situation when booking.

  • Do I need current registration?

    Not required. We accept vehicles with expired registration or no plates. However, you must be the legal owner. If transferring ownership, we'll need signed paperwork from the previous owner.

  • Can you remove multiple vehicles?

    Yes! We offer fleet removal services for 2+ vehicles. Receive volume discounts and coordinated pickup times. Ideal for clearing dealership overstocks, inherited vehicles, or company fleets.

  • Do I need to remove personal items?

    Please remove all personal belongings before pickup. While we do a final check, we're not responsible for items left in vehicles. Valuable parts (stereos, wheels) can be removed but may affect valuation.
